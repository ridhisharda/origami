INTERESTING FACTS ABOUT CAMEL

  • There are two types of camels: One humped or “dromedary” camels and two humped Bactrian camels.
  • Camels have three sets of eyelids and two rows of eyelashes to keep sand out of their eyes.
  • Camels have thick lips which let them forage for thorny plants other animals can't eat.

INTERESTING FACTS ABOUT PANDA

  • Giant pandas are good at climbing trees and can also swim.
  • Pandas have so many fans because they look cute.
  • A giant panda is much bigger than your teddy bear.

INTERESTING FACTS ABOUT CHAMELEON

  • Most chameleons have a prehensile tail that they use to wrap around tree branches.
  • Chameleons are a very unique branch of the lizard group of reptiles.
  • Their hands and feet have large toes that help them cling to branches.

INTERESTING FACTS ABOUT CICADA

  • Cicadas live in temperate to tropical climates where they are one of the most widely recognized of all insects.
  • Cicadas are among the loudest insects known to man, and a swarm of them can produce sounds up to 120 decibels.
  • Cicadas can be black, brown or green and can have red, white or blue eyes.

INTERESTING FACTS ABOUT PIGEON

  • Pigeons are incredibly complex and intelligent animals.
  • Pigeons are renowned for their outstanding navigational abilities.
  • Pigeons mate for life, and tend to raise two chicks at the same time.
